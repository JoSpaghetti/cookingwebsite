Chicken and Rice Recipe

Time: 1-1.5 hour(s)

Servings: 6 portions


By: Joseph "Chef Joe" W.

Instagram: @ChefJoe4568

Ingredients Needed:

For the chicken marinade:

  • 1/4th cup Greek Plain Yogurt
  • 1 Lime (or Lemon)
  • 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Garam Masala
  • 1/2th tablespoon Garlic+Ginger paste
  • 1/4th tablespoon Turmeric
  • 1/4th tablespoon Cumin Powder
  • 1/4th tablespoon Cumin Seeds
  • A pinch of Salt + Pepper
  • 2 lbs Chicken drumsticks or thighs
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 spicy pepper (Use a pepper who’s intensity you can handle)
  • 2lbs tomatoes (or 500ml of Tomato Sauce)
  • 1 can of coconut milk

Optional:

  • 1-2 medium carrot(s) - adds sweetness
  • Rice (preferably Basmati Rice, but any works)

Recipe:

  1. Mix wet elements of chicken marinade in a container
  2. Mix dry elements of chicken marinade in a container
  3. Sprinkle dry marinade on raw chicken
  4. Coat chicken in wet marinade
  5. Rest chicken in covered bowl overnight in fridge
  6. Cut large onion, green pepper, (optionally carrots)and spicy pepper into strips
  7. Preheat pan on stove on medium high with olive oil
  8. Remove chicken from fridge and container before pan frying
  9. Cook until color forms on one side, then turn over
  10. Once most of the chickens exterior has developed color, remove chicken and set aside
    (Cook in batches so the pan isn’t overcrowded)
  11. Cook onions until sweating, then add peppers until browned in color
    (optional) Add carrots and saute until fragrant
  12. Add tomato sauce and coconut milk to pan
  13. Once sauce is boiling, add chicken to pan and lower heat to medium low
  14. Simmer for 40 mins to 1 hour before removing pan from heat
  15. Let chicken settle for 30 mins, grab a plate of rice, and serve!
